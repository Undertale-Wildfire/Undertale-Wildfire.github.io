incessantAlchemist

Would I ever spare a human?

Such a question could only be conceived out of naïvety.

To ask such a question... the only conclusion that I can reach...

... is that your understanding of the Underground must be nonexistent.

Tell me, would you allow a plague to enter your village,

knowing full well that it would lead to the death of your neighbors?

Of all of your friends, family, those whom you hold dear?

Unless you truly are a fool, you would not.

A threat to your livelihood should not be ignored.

It most certainly should not be shown even a single iota of mercy.

Doing so would be dangerous, and dare I say, even selfish.

A monster's duty should be to protect and guard our way of life.

To ensure monsterkind's survival, and pave the way for a bright and shining future.

So, no.

No, I would not.

Humans are not creatures worthy of our compassion.
